About us

State Drug And Alcohol Rehab Directory

Addiction to drugs or alcohol is a devastating chronic disease both recovery from addiction is possible with many different treatment options available. Some of the major categories of treatment are Residential Rehabs, where the addict is supervised 24 hours a day/7 days a week, Outpatient Treatment, where the individual attends meetings and counselling, but is not required to live in the rehab facility and Inpatient Hospital Treatment.
